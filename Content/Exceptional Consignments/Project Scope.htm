Objectives

  1. Data Verification: This guide will provide a comprehensive instruction on how to verify data related to the goods and the wagons from the documents provided by clients. This includes the inspection of the type of goods, their quantity, weight, dimensions, and other relevant details. The same applies to the inspection of the wagons, including their type, capacity, suitability for the goods, and other relevant details.
  2. Data Application: This includes determining the most suitable wagons for specific goods, planning the transport route, and transport organization to ensure compliance with all relevant regulations and safety standards.

  3. Exceptional Transport: The project will focus on exceptional consignments, which involve goods that are oversized, overweight, or require special handling. The application will provide specific guidance for these types of consignments, ensuring that they are transported safely, efficiently, and in compliance with all regulations.

Expected Outcomes

With the help of this user guide, users should be able to:

  • Understand how to verify the quality of the data related to goods, wagons and paths.
  • Decide whether relevant data for application is sufficient for an exceptional transport application.
  • Ask client for the missing data, if any.
  • Fill up the application form effectively and in compliance with all relevant regulations.

Conclusion

This project aims to make the process of planning and executing exceptional consignments more efficient and less prone to errors. By providing clear guidance on data verification and provision of comprehensive data in the application for exceptional transport permit, it will help users ensure that their consignments are transported safely and efficiently.
