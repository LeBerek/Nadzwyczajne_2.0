Other Exceptional Consignments

While out-of-gauge and heavy exceptional consignments are well-defined and straightforward, the third category of Other Exceptional Consignments at first sight is more elusive. It encompasses a diverse range of goods and vehicles qualities that defy a single, meaningful common description.

Some of these consignments pose unique challenges due to goods nature and the method of loading, arrangement and securing while on the wagons. Another distinct aspect focuses on authorizations conditions for a wide range of vehicles running or towed on their own wheels.
