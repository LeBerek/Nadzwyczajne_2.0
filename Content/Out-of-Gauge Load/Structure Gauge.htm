Structure Gauge

Static Method

For consignments exceeds the gauge, the possibility of its transport should be checked, taking into account, among other things:

  • load distribution on the wagon,
  • critical points of the consignment (wagon and/or load),
  • lateral deviations of the longitudinal axis of the wagon
  • individual critical points of the consignment from the track axis in curves,
  • operational deviations resulting from the interaction of track and rolling stock. The basic radius of the curve is taken as R = 250 m.
